WHAT WE DO

Medical Assessments

Independent Medical Examinations

A comprehensive assessment answering specific medical-legal questions, including file review and clinical interview. Ideal for legal disputes, insurance claims, and disability determinations.

Medical File Review

A structured review of medical records addressing diagnosis and treatment appropriateness without an in-person exam. Useful when claimants are unavailable or a second opinion is needed.

Functional and Work Capacity Opinions

Objective evaluation of a person's ability to perform work-related tasks, identifying limitations and recovery timelines. Helps plan return-to-work strategies and workplace accommodations.

Return-to-Work Assessments

Evidence-based determination of when and how an individual can safely return to employment, including functional restrictions and timelines. Ensures a structured approach to reintegration into the workplace.

Expert Medical Reports

Structured, defensible medical-legal reports summarizing referral questions and clinical findings. Professionally written to meet legal and insurance expectations.
